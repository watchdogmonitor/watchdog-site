Watchdog Device

Local-only temperature & humidity monitoring.

Watchdog is a plug-and-play environmental monitoring appliance built on Raspberry Pi hardware. It uses Bluetooth sensors to track temperature and humidity and sends email alerts when conditions go out of range.

Runs entirely on your local network. No cloud. No subscription.

Why it exists

Most consumer monitors rely on cloud services and mobile apps. If the company shuts down or the cloud service fails, the device stops being useful.

Watchdog was built as a self-contained appliance that runs locally and continues operating independently of external services.

Who it's for

  • Reptile enclosures
  • Orchid grow tents
  • Bonsai winter storage
  • Wine cellars
  • Small labs

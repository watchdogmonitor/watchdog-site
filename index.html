Watchdog Device

Local-only temperature & humidity monitoring.

Watchdog is a plug-and-play environmental monitor built on Raspberry Pi. It uses Bluetooth sensors to track temperature and humidity and sends email alerts when conditions go out of range.

Runs entirely on your local network. No cloud. No subscription.

Who it's for

  • Reptile enclosures
  • Orchid grow tents
  • Bonsai winter storage
  • Wine cellars
  • Small labs
